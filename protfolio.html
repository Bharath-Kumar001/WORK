About Me

I'm an Embedded Systems Engineer with a passion for real-time control systems. My journey began with an intensive 8-month advanced embedded development program at Vector India, where I gained a strong foundation in Embedded C programming, microcontroller architecture, and peripheral interfacing.

I developed proficiency in working with ARM-based processors (including STM32 microcontrollers) and learned to interface sensors using I2C, SPI, UART, and GPIO protocols. My training also covered Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS), mastering concepts like task management, inter-task communication, and time-critical operations.

Following my training, I joined Gridbots Technologies in Ahmedabad as a Trainee Embedded Engineer, where I applied my skills to develop robotic systems, focusing on motion control, sensor integration, and system debugging. My work also involved contributing to IoT-based solutions and real-time data processing.

I'm particularly interested in embedded systems, where precise timing, efficient resource management, and robust hardware-software integration come together to create intelligent systems.
